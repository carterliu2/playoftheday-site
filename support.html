Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enable or disable notifications?

Open the App, go to Settings, and toggle "Daily pick alerts" on or off. You can also manage notifications in your device's system settings.

Is the App free?

Yes. Play of the Day is completely free with no in-app purchases or subscriptions.

Does the App place bets for me?

No. Play of the Day provides AI-generated sports analysis for entertainment purposes only. It does not facilitate, process, or enable real-money gambling or wagering of any kind.

How do I delete my data?

Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data. To request deletion of server-side data (device identifier and preferences), email privacy@playoftheday.app.

What data does the App collect?

Only an anonymous device identifier, push notification token, and your sport/bet-type preferences. No personal information is collected. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
